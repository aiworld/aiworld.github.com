The goal of AiWorld is to prepare for and accelerate the emergence of artificial intelligence in a safe and open way. AiWorld aims to be a platform where researchers can build, test, and collaborate on sensorimotor algorithms out in the open. In addition, it will be a playable game where anyone can interact with and train AI's, including a series of tests, standards, and competitions that ascertain an AI's ability to function within our society.

Stay tuned for more on how you can play a part in creating the future of artificial intelligence.
